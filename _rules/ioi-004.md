## Dependency specification

1. **$MFT** — files under `System Volume Information`: infrastructure files (`tracking.log`, `IndexerVolumeGuid`, `_OnDiskSnapshotProp`) and GUID/Apps directories
2. **$UsnJrnl** — `FileDelete` and `FileDeleteClose` records matching GUID-format or `Apps_{}` filenames

## Invariant predicate φ

If VSS infrastructure files exist under `System Volume Information`, the corresponding GUID snapshot directories and `Apps_{}` subdirectories must also be present.

## Contradiction condition ¬φ

Fires when infrastructure files are present **and** no GUID or `Apps_{}` directories appear in the MFT **and** USN records confirm their deletion.

## SPARQL signature

The rule uses two `UNION` branches — one for `Apps_{...}` directories and one for 38-character GUID-format names — each paired with `FILTER NOT EXISTS` against the MFT graph and a USN corroboration lookup. Full query in `rules/sparql/ioi-004.rq`.
